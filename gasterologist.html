GASTROENTEROLOGIST

A gastroenterologist takes care of all the abnormalities in the activities of your digestive organs such as cancer, hepatitis, gastroesophageal reflux (heartburn), peptic ulcer disease, colitis, gallbladder and biliary tract disease, nutritional problems, Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS), and pancreatitis. Also a Gastroenterologists performs a number of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures including colonoscopy, endoscopy, endoscopic retrograde cholangiancreatography (ERCP), endoscopic ultrasound and liver biopsy. So, to sum up any digestive disorder should land you with a visit to a Gastroenterologist.
