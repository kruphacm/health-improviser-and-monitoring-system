ONCOLOGIST

An oncologist is a doctor who treats cancer and provides medical care for a person diagnosed with cancer. An oncologist may also be called a cancer specialist. hey often act as the main healthcare provider for someone with cancer—designing treatment plans, offering supportive care, and sometimes coordinating treatment with other specialists.The field of oncology has 3 major areas based on treatments: medical oncology, radiation oncology, and surgical oncology.
